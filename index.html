By-line

What is Mambo-MS?

Mambo-MS is Metabolomics Australia's (MA) central library of metabolites. It aimed to assist in the interpretation of hyphenated mass spectrometry (gas chromatography-mass spectrometry/liquid chromatography mass spectrometry) metabolomics experiments.

Mambo-MS Modules

User Management Module

The User management system consists of managing different user groups such as System Administrator, Node Representative, MA Lab Staff and Clients, each of who posses different privileges in accessing the database.


Store of metabolite records

The metabolite records that are stored in Mambo-MS consists of:

  • NIST database that is made up of more than 250,000 records (local only to MA);
  • GC-MS records that are generated at MA Nodes; and
  • LC-MS records generated at MA Nodes.


Search and visualisation of metabolite records

The search component of Mambo-MS is made up of keyword based search and mass spectrum based search.
